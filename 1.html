HTML


A markup language for describing the appearance of a website . It is not a programming language , but a markup language that expresses markup information, including the structure or format of documents other than the contents of documents. As you can see, in the first place, the name HTML means ML is a markup language.

htm or html extensions commonly seen on websites are documents written in this language. The original proposer was CERN physicist Timothy J. Berners-Lee . URL , HTTP , and Enquire, the predecessor of WWW , were also developed and proposed by him as a set. He is called the father of the Internet along with Vinton Gray Cerf, who created the TCP/IP communication protocol.
